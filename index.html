About the Firm

A Legacy of Legal Excellence in South Africa

NT Shabangu and Associates is a full-service law firm committed to providing high-quality legal services to individuals, businesses, and organisations across South Africa. We believe that access to justice is a right, not a privilege.

Our firm is built on the principle that every client deserves dedicated, personalised legal counsel — whether navigating a complex commercial matter, a property transaction, or a sensitive family dispute.

We bring a practical, results-oriented approach to every matter we handle, ensuring our clients are informed, empowered, and protected at every step of their legal journey.

Our People

Meet the Principal Attorney

NT

N.T. Shabangu

Principal Attorney · Notary · Conveyancer

N.T. Shabangu is an admitted attorney of the High Court of South Africa with over fifteen years of experience in a wide range of legal disciplines. Known for a forthright yet empathetic approach, he brings intellectual rigour and strategic insight to every mandate.

Having served clients from individuals and small businesses to established corporations, he understands that legal matters often come at moments of great personal or professional stress — and is committed to providing clear, actionable guidance that makes a real difference.

He is a member of the Law Society of South Africa and is admitted as a Notary and Conveyancer, allowing the firm to handle the full spectrum of property and notarial matters.
